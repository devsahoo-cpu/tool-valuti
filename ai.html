Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the AI Art Detector?
Our AI Art Detector has a 92% accuracy rate in distinguishing between human-created and AI-generated artwork based on extensive testing with thousands of verified images.
What file types does the AI Art Detector support?
The tool supports JPG, PNG, and SVG files up to 20MB in size. For best results, use high-quality images without heavy compression.
How does the AI Art Detector work?
The detector analyzes various aspects of the image including texture patterns, color distribution, brush strokes, and compositional elements to determine the likelihood of AI generation. Our algorithm compares these features against known characteristics of both human-created and AI-generated artwork.
Is my uploaded artwork stored or used for training?
No, we do not store your uploaded images permanently. All analysis is performed in real-time and your artwork is deleted from our servers immediately after processing.
