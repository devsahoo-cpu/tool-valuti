Frequently Asked Questions

Is my task data really private?
Yes, ForgetfulTasks Pro stores all your data locally in your browser with optional end-to-end encrypted sync. Nothing is stored on our servers in readable form.
How does the AI suggestion feature work?
Our AI analyzes your task completion patterns, time of day productivity, and task types to offer personalized suggestions for improving your workflow.
Can I access my tasks from multiple devices?
Yes, with our optional encrypted sync feature (requires account), your tasks are securely available across all your devices while maintaining privacy.
